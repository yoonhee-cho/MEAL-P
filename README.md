<h2><p align="center"> Efficient meal plan, grocery shopping, recipe managing tool</p></h2>

<!-- Plan your meals, manage your grocery shopping list within your budget, get meal plan ideas, and store your own recipes !  -->
## ⭐️ Main Features <br>
1. Write a grocery shopping list based on the menus on your meal plan <br/>
2. Adjust the items based on the estimated price within your budget<br/>
3. Search recipes by ingredients to get meal plan ideas<br/>
4. Post your own recipes you want to keep<br/>
